# Dog Park Meetup Launch Kit

## Positioning

Dog Park Meetup is the local community board for dog parks. It helps owners say when they are going, see who else is headed there, and choose times when dogs can meet familiar playmates.

## Core Message

Make the dog park feel like a community.

## Taglines

- Plan visits. Find your local pack.
- Know who is going before you go.
- Turn dog park trips into meetups.
- Meet the people and dogs already headed to your park.

## Primary Ad

Headline: Make the dog park feel like a community.

Body: Dog Park Meetup helps nearby owners post planned visits, find overlapping schedules, and meet the local dogs who play at the same parks.

CTA: Plan a meetup

## Short Social Ads

1. Heading: Is anyone going to the dog park tonight?
   Copy: Check Dog Park Meetup before you leave. See planned visits, dog profiles, and community notes from nearby owners.
   CTA: Find your park

2. Heading: Your dog park has a local pack.
   Copy: Dog Park Meetup helps you find the owners and dogs who already visit your favorite parks.
   CTA: Join the meetup

3. Heading: Stop guessing when the park is social.
   Copy: Post your plan, see who is going, and show up when playmates are more likely to be there.
   CTA: Plan your visit

## App Store Description

Dog Park Meetup is a community app for dog park regulars. Search local parks, post when you plan to go, see upcoming visits from other owners, and coordinate better play windows for your dog. Share park conditions, manage your dogs, message owners after matching visits, and build a friendlier local dog park routine.

## SEO Title

Dog Park Meetup - Plan dog park visits with nearby owners

## SEO Description

Dog Park Meetup helps dog owners plan park visits, find overlapping schedules, see who is going, and connect with the local dog park community.

## Launch Channels

- Local dog park flyer with QR code and the headline "Know who is going before you go."
- Neighborhood Facebook groups and Nextdoor posts focused on specific parks.
- Vet offices, groomers, trainers, shelters, and dog daycare partners.
- Park-specific social posts: "Lake Atalanta owners, plan tonight's meetup."
- App store screenshots showing planned visits, compatibility badges, community notes, and messages.
